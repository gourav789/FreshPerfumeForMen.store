Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FreshPerfumeForMen.store collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy in line with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Our site provides honest reviews, buying guides, and recommendations for fresh perfumes for men across the United Kingdom. We collect only the information necessary to improve your experience and deliver relevant content.

We operate as a content-focused website and do not sell physical products directly. Any purchases you make through affiliate links on our site are handled by third-party retailers. This policy applies to all visitors, including those browsing our detailed fragrance reviews and seasonal guides.

Introduction

FreshPerfumeForMen.store is dedicated to helping men in the United Kingdom discover fresh fragrances that suit British weather, professional environments, and everyday life. We take data protection seriously and aim to be transparent about how we handle information. This policy outlines what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have under UK law.

We review our practices regularly to ensure they remain fair and lawful. If you have any questions after reading this policy, please contact us using the details provided at the end.

Information We Collect

We collect two main types of information: personal data and non-personal data. Personal data identifies you as an individual, while non-personal data does not. We only gather what is necessary for the operation of the site and to provide useful content about fresh perfumes for men.

Most of the information we collect comes from your interactions with the website, such as reading reviews or using search functions. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details to browse our articles on topics like office fragrances or budget-friendly options.

Personal Information

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it. This typically happens if you contact us via email with a question about a specific fragrance review, suggest a new perfume for future coverage, or provide feedback on our buying guides.

Examples of personal information include your name, email address, and any details you choose to share in your message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry. For instance, if you ask about how a particular fresh perfume performs during rainy British afternoons, we may use your email to send a helpful reply.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as health information or financial details unless you choose to share it in a message, which we advise against for security reasons.

Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information includes technical data that helps us understand how visitors use the site. This may include your IP address (anonymised where possible), browser type, device information, and pages visited. We use this data to improve site performance, fix technical issues, and understand which of our fragrance guides are most popular with UK readers.

For example, we may see that many visitors from Scotland read our seasonal recommendations during cooler months. This helps us refine future content without identifying individual users.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help the site function properly and remember your preferences. We use both essential cookies, which are necessary for basic site operation, and optional cookies for analytics and advertising.

Essential cookies allow you to navigate smoothly between our perfume reviews and guides. Optional cookies help us understand visitor behaviour so we can improve recommendations for fresh fragrances suited to UK conditions.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. We provide clear information about cookie use so you can make informed choices.

Analytics

We use analytics tools to gather non-personal data about how people interact with FreshPerfumeForMen.store. This includes information on which pages receive the most visits, such as our comprehensive reviews of luxury or budget fresh perfumes, and how long readers spend on buying guides.

Analytics help us identify popular topics, such as office-friendly fragrances or options that perform well in mild British summers. The data is aggregated and does not identify individual visitors. We use it solely to enhance the quality and relevance of our content for UK audiences.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. This service tracks page views, session duration, and general geographic information (such as country or city level) without identifying you personally. Google Analytics uses cookies and may process your IP address in an anonymised form.

The information helps us understand which fragrance topics interest British readers most, allowing us to focus on useful guides and reviews. Google’s privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking through browser extensions or settings.

Google AdSense

Some pages on our site may display advertisements through Google AdSense. These ads are contextual and based on the content you are viewing, such as articles about fresh perfumes for men. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads and measure their performance.

We do not control the specific ads displayed and have no access to personal data collected by Google for advertising purposes. You can manage ad personalisation through your Google account settings or by using ad-blocking tools if preferred.

Affiliate Links

Our website includes affiliate links to retailers selling the fragrances we review. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the retailer may place cookies on your device to track the referral. This allows us to earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate links help support the running of FreshPerfumeForMen.store. The use of these links does not affect our editorial opinions or recommendations. We only include links to products we believe offer genuine value for UK readers based on performance in British conditions.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the website effectively. These may include hosting providers, email services for responding to enquiries, and content delivery networks. These services process data only on our instructions and under strict confidentiality agreements.

We choose partners who demonstrate strong data protection standards. Any data shared with third parties is limited to what is necessary for the service they provide, such as delivering our fragrance review pages quickly across the UK.

Embedded Content

Our articles may include embedded content from other websites, such as short video clips demonstrating fragrance application or links to external resources. When you view embedded content, that third-party site may collect data about your visit using its own cookies and tracking technologies.

We embed content only when it adds clear value to our readers, such as visual explanations of perfume notes. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit through embeds.

Email Communication

When you contact us by email, we collect the information you provide to respond appropriately. This may include your email address and the content of your message, such as a question about a specific fresh perfume review or feedback on our seasonal recommendations.

We use this information only to reply to your enquiry and do not add you to marketing lists without your consent. Email communications are stored securely and deleted once the matter is resolved, unless longer retention is required for legal reasons.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. These include secure servers, access controls, and regular security reviews.

While no system is completely risk-free, we follow industry best practices to safeguard information. In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by UK GDPR.

Children's Privacy

FreshPerfumeForMen.store is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under this age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action to remove it.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have several rights regarding your personal data. These include the right to access the data we hold about you, request corrections if information is inaccurate, and ask for deletion of your data in certain circumstances.

You may also object to processing, request restriction of processing, and ask for your data to be provided in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within the timeframes set by law, usually one month.

UK GDPR Rights

As a UK-based site serving visitors in the United Kingdom, we comply fully with UK GDPR requirements. This includes having a lawful basis for processing your data, such as your consent for optional cookies or our legitimate interest in improving the site.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) if you believe your data protection rights have been breached. We encourage you to contact us first so we can address any concerns directly.

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though doing so may affect how some parts of the site function. We provide clear notices about cookie use when you first visit so you can make an informed choice.

For Google Analytics and advertising cookies, you can use Google’s own opt-out tools or browser extensions designed for privacy management. We respect your choices and do not use cookies for purposes beyond those described.

Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites, including retailer pages through affiliate programmes and resources mentioned in our fragrance guides. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. For example, email enquiry details are kept until the matter is resolved and for a short period afterwards for record-keeping. Analytics data is typically retained in aggregated form for a limited time to support site improvements.

We regularly review our data holdings and delete information that is no longer required.

International Data Transfers

Some of the third-party services we use, such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense, are based outside the United Kingdom. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses approved by the UK authorities.

These measures help protect your data to a standard equivalent to UK GDPR requirements.
